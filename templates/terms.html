History

The spread of COVID-19 is increasingly worrying everyone in the world. This virus can be affected from human to human through the droplets and airborne. According to the guidelines provided from WHO, to reduce the spread of COVID-19, every people need to wear face mask, social distancing, avoiding the crowd areas and also maintaining our immune system. Therefore, to protect each other, every person should wear face masks properly when they are in outdoors. However, most of selfish and careless people won't wear masks properly for so many reasons. To overcome this situation, a robust face mask detection needs to be developed. And hence in these situation balaclava can be used. Accidents and high death rates on construction sites are common these days and with that comes the necessity of wearing safety gaurds like hard hats and also to ensure that everyone is wearing them for which again can use balaclava.
